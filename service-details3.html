An industrial cold room is a specialized refrigerated storage space that caters to harsh industrial environments. It creates a temperature-controlled environment that ensures products stored in them remain fresh and stable. Depending on the client’s requirements, industrial cold rooms can create an ideal storage environment of 0°C to -30°C. They can come in various forms, including chill cold rooms and freezers.
